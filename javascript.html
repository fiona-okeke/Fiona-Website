My JavaScript Journey

JavaScript presented a unique challenge in my coding journey. Despite being forewarned about its complexity, I underestimated the magnitude of its difficulty. The trusted YouTuber whose tutorials had been my guiding light through HTML and CSS fell short in elucidating the intricacies of JavaScript. Desperate for guidance, I scoured through countless resources, only to be met with a daunting three-hour tutorial that left me feeling overwhelmed. Faced with this daunting prospect, I realized that my learning approach needed to adapt. Instead of a structured curriculum, I embraced a more spontaneous method, seeking answers to specific questions as they arose. However, my breakthrough came with the discovery of FreeCodeCamp, a platform that revolutionised my learning experience. With FreeCodeCamp, concepts that once seemed insurmountable began to crystallise, and witnessing my creations come to life with functional precision was nothing short of exhilarating.
