My HTML Journey

I embarked on my journey with HTML late last year, before the mention of Founders and Coders graced my ears. Little did I know, this venture into the realm of coding would ignite an unexpected passion within me. HTML captured my fascination with its ability to transform lines of code into tangible, living creations. As someone rooted in the field of journalism, the prospect of crafting a website for my documentary production company felt like a serendipitous alignment of my interests and aspirations.

Witnessing the metamorphosis from mere concepts to fully-fledged web elements filled me with a sense of fulfillment like no other. Each form created, every headline meticulously crafted, symbolized progress and potential. Initially, my foray into HTML was guided by online tutorials, with YouTube serving as my sole instructor. Consistency proved elusive as life's demands pulled me in various directions, relegating my coding endeavors to brief, sporadic sessions.

However, with the dawn of 2024 came a renewed resolve for discipline. The looming deadline of February 26th served as a poignant reminder of my aspirations, reigniting my determination to delve back into the world of HTML. Reflecting on the journey thus far, I can confidently affirm that the process of learning HTML has been a source of genuine enjoyment and personal growth.
